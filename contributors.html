the voices of naissance

photo

amara obi

Amara Obi is a Nigerian-American writer based in Brooklyn. Her work has appeared in The Paris Review, Granta, and One Story. She is currently working on her first novel about memory and migration.

photo

james whitfield

James Whitfield lives in Portland, Oregon, where he teaches creative writing at Portland State. His poetry collection, "Small Hours," was a finalist for the National Book Award.

photo

mei-ling chang

Mei-Ling Chang is a Taiwanese writer and translator. Her essays explore diaspora, language, and the body. She holds an MFA from the University of Michigan.

photo

carlos mendez

Carlos Mendez was born in Bogotá and raised in Miami. His short stories have appeared in McSweeney's and ZYZZYVA. He works as a carpenter when he's not writing.

photo

fatima hassan

Fatima Hassan is a poet and visual artist from Nairobi. Her work interrogates borders, both literal and metaphorical. She is a Cave Canem fellow.

photo

ben kaufman

Ben Kaufman writes fiction and criticism from Chicago. His work often examines the intersections of technology and intimacy. He is allergic to cats but owns two.
