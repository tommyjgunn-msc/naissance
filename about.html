about us

naissance—from the French word meaning birth or origin—is a literary magazine dedicated to publishing fiction, poetry, and creative nonfiction that captures moments of transformation. We believe in the power of language to illuminate the spaces between what was and what is becoming.

Founded in 2024, we emerged from a simple conviction: that there are stories waiting in the margins, voices humming beneath the static, narratives that deserve a place to unfold. We are particularly interested in work that challenges conventional forms, that sits comfortably in discomfort, that asks questions it cannot answer.

We publish quarterly, each issue organized around a loose thematic thread. We pay our contributors, because we believe art is labor and labor deserves compensation. We are committed to amplifying emerging voices alongside established ones, believing that the conversation between generations enriches us all.

submissions

We read year-round and respond within three months. We accept previously unpublished work only. Simultaneous submissions are welcome—please withdraw promptly if your work is accepted elsewhere.

fiction: up to 7,000 words. we're drawn to stories that trust the reader, that leave room for ambiguity, that find the strange in the familiar.

poetry: up to five poems per submission. we admire precision, music, and images that linger. send us your weird ones, your quiet ones, your unresolved ones.

creative nonfiction: up to 5,000 words. essays, memoir, hybrid forms. we want truth that reads like invention.

We pay $100 for fiction, $50 per poem, and $75 for nonfiction. Submit via our Submittable portal.
